Chicken Tikka Masala

A popular Indian dish made with marinated chicken in a spiced tomato sauce.

Serving Size: 4

Prep Time: 15 mins | Cook Time: 30 mins

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast, cubed
  • 200g yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons tikka masala paste
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Equipment Needed

  • Large mixing bowl
  • Frying pan
  • Wooden spoon
  • Knife and chopping board

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken in yogurt and tikka masala paste for at least 1 hour.
  2. In a pan, sauté the onion and garlic until soft.
  3. Add the marinated chicken and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in the canned tomatoes and simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, and serve with rice or naan.

Vegetable Stir-Fry

A quick and healthy dish packed with colorful vegetables and a savory sauce.

Serving Size: 4

Prep Time: 10 mins | Cook Time: 15 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, carrots)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Equipment Needed

  • Wok or large frying pan
  • Spatula
  • Knife and chopping board

Instructions

  1. Heat sesame oil in a wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add garlic and ginger, sauté for 1 minute.
  3. Add mixed vegetables and stir-fry for 5-7 minutes until tender-crisp.
  4. Pour in soy sauce and stir to combine.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, and serve hot.

Grilled Steak

A juicy grilled steak seasoned to perfection, perfect for any occasion.

Serving Size: 2

Prep Time: 10 mins | Cook Time: 15 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 ribeye steaks
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon rosemary, chopped

Equipment Needed

  • Grill or grill pan
  • Tongs
  • Meat thermometer
  • Cutting board

Instructions

  1. Preheat the grill to high heat.
  2. Rub the steaks with olive oil, garlic powder, rosemary, salt, and pepper.
  3. Place the steaks on the grill and cook for 4-5 minutes on each side for medium-rare.
  4. Use a meat thermometer to check for desired doneness (130°F for medium-rare).
  5. Remove from the grill and let rest for 5 minutes before slicing.
